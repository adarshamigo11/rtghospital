About RTG Hospital

Committed to excellence in orthopaedic and multispeciality healthcare — now at two locations

Our Story

Redhills RTG Hospital (Right Time / Right Care / Right Place) is a dedicated bone, joint, and multispeciality hospital. Originally established at No. 514, GNT Road, Redhills, Chennai — 600052, we have now expanded with a second branch at Taharpur Road, GTB Enclave, Dilshad Garden, New Delhi — 110095.

We specialize in minimally invasive orthopaedic surgeries, trauma care, diabetic management, and general medicine. With 7+ years of service, 36+ medical professionals, and 50+ beds, RTG Hospital delivers quality healthcare close to home — in both Chennai and Delhi.

Our mission: the right care, at the right time, in the right place — for every patient.
